About the Foundation

Continuing a legacy of leadership, integrity, and community impact through purposeful action and unwavering commitment to positive change.

Our Foundation Story

The SBO Foundation was established in 2018 to honor and continue the remarkable legacy of Simeon Babafemi Olaleye (1928-2018), affectionately known as Pa SBO. After 90 years of impactful living, Pa SBO left behind a testament to what one person can achieve through unwavering dedication to family, community, and moral excellence.

Founded by his children and loved ones, the Foundation serves as a living memorial that transforms grief into purpose, memories into action, and love into lasting change. We believe that the best way to honor Pa SBO's memory is to ensure his values continue to shape lives and communities for generations to come.

What began as a family's desire to preserve their father's legacy has evolved into a beacon of hope for young leaders, students, and communities who share Pa SBO's vision for a better world.

Foundation Building

🎯 Our Mission

To perpetuate the legacy of Simeon Babafemi Olaleye by supporting educational initiatives, youth development programs, and community projects that reflect his values of leadership, integrity, and service. We create sustainable impact through strategic investments in people and communities, ensuring that Pa SBO's principles continue to inspire positive change across generations.

🌟 Our Vision

A world where every individual is empowered to lead with integrity, serve with purpose, and create lasting positive change in their communities. We envision thriving societies built on the foundation of strong character, educational excellence, and unwavering commitment to the common good—values that defined Pa SBO's remarkable life.

Our Core Values

👨‍💼

Leadership

We inspire others through example, fostering the next generation of ethical leaders who serve with humility and purpose. True leadership is about empowering others to reach their full potential while maintaining the highest standards of character.

🤝

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and moral principles in all our endeavors. Every decision we make is guided by ethical considerations and a commitment to doing what is right, even when it's difficult.

🌟

Impact

We create meaningful, lasting change that transforms lives and strengthens communities. Our programs are designed not just to provide immediate assistance, but to build sustainable pathways to success and self-reliance.
