Our Mission

The SBO Foundation exists to perpetuate the legacy of Simeon Babafemi Olaleye by supporting educational initiatives, youth development programs, and community projects that reflect his values of leadership, integrity, and service.

We believe that by investing in people and communities, we can create a ripple effect of positive change that honors Pa SBO's memory and continues his work for generations to come.
