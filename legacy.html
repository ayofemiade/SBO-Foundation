A Life of Purpose and Service

Simeon Babafemi Olaleye was more than a father, leader, and community figure ‚Äì he was a beacon of hope whose light continues to shine through the lives he touched and the values he instilled.

Born in 1928, Pa SBO lived through nearly a century of remarkable change, yet his core principles remained steadfast: integrity in all dealings, leadership through service, and the unwavering belief that every individual has the potential to make a positive difference in the world.

Throughout his 90 years, Pa SBO demonstrated that true wealth lies not in material possessions, but in the relationships we build, the lives we impact, and the legacy we leave behind. He was a mentor to many, a pillar of strength for his family, and a respected voice in his community.

His approach to life was simple yet profound: lead by example, treat others with dignity and respect, and never stop learning or growing. These principles guided him through various roles as a father, professional, and community leader, earning him the respect and admiration of all who knew him.

Pa SBO's legacy is not just in the accomplishments he achieved, but in the values he passed on and the generations he continues to inspire. His life serves as a testament to the power of consistent character, purposeful living, and the profound impact one person can have on the world.
